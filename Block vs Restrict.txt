Block {
--> They will know that the person has blocked them.
--> Will not see posts eventhough they searched the person who blocked them, 
    only the numbers will be shown. Will not be notified about the person's activities.
--> Can not message that person.
--> Can not tag them.
}

Restrict {
--> They will not know that the person has restricted them.
--> Will see posts, can comment on the posts but other person will not see the comments.
--> Person will not be notified about the comments and message. Person have to manually check it.
--> Can tag them.
}
